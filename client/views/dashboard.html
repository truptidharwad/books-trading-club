 settings Update Settings How This Works

Add books you own and are willing to trade through the Google Books search. Then visit Everyone's Books to find books you would like to add to your collection via trades with members. On the Trades page:

  1. Pending trades will appear when a user requests a book you own.
  2. Click on Initiate Trade if you wish to move forward with a request in your Pending list.
  3. Once initiated, both users will be provided with each other's contact info to arrange the trade.
  4. The book's owner can cancel the trade, which returns the book to their collection, and to the other's Books I Want list.
  5. Click the 'Trade Complete' button once you receive a book, to close off the trade.

Note: Your details kept private by default. Personal is shared only with users who have the books you have indicated you want. All trades must be arrange via email - this site does not provide any means for users to communicate with one another.
